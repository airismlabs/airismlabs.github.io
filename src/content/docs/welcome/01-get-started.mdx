## Welcome to Airism

Airism helps you unlock the value hidden in your AI conversation exports. Whether you have months of ChatGPT conversations or detailed Claude discussions, Airism transforms messy export files into organized, searchable, and actionable data.

## What is Airism?

Airism consists of two complementary products:

- **Kit**: A TypeScript library for parsing and normalizing AI conversation exports
- **Studio**: A web application for visualizing, exploring, and analyzing your conversations

## Overview of Kit and Studio

### Airism Kit
A type-safe, composable API that transforms raw AI exports into structured data. Perfect for developers building automations, dashboards, or custom analysis tools.

### Airism Studio  
An intuitive web interface that lets you explore your AI conversations through timeline views, advanced search, tagging, and cross-session insights.
